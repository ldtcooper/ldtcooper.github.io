About Me

While I'm currently a graduate student working on my MS in Economics and Computation at Duke University, I'm also a software engineer and data scientist with experience at companies both large and small. My main languages are Python and JavaScript, although I have some experience in Java, Ruby, and Stata. I'm also trying to add Haskell to that toolbelt. My main interests are in how we can apply the tools of data science to making better public policy, especially in the transit and finance. I'm going to be finishing up my masters in May 2023 and I'm currently open to work after that. If my experience sounds like a good match for you, feel free to get in touch!
